Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What dice can I roll?

Awesome Dice Roller! supports the standard polyhedral set: d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, and d20. You can roll up to 10 dice in a single throw.

Is there a subscription or in-app purchases?

Nope. Awesome Dice Roller! is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no ads, no surprise fees. Updates are included.

How do I roll the dice?

Two ways — give your device a shake to roll like the real thing, or tap the screen for a quieter throw.

Can I customize dice colors?

Yes! Every die can be customized to the color of your choice, letting you build a personalized set that looks exactly right.

Does the app keep a history of my rolls?

It sure does. Your recent throws are saved so you can review them anytime — handy for settling disputes or reliving your best rolls.

What devices does it work on?

Awesome Dice Roller! is available on iPhone and iPad through the App Store.
