Privacy Policy

App: Game Dice
Effective date: April 29, 2026
Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Game Dice (the "App") handles information when you use it. We respect your privacy and have designed the App to function without collecting personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Game Dice does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. The App does not require an account, does not request your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal data.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores certain non-personal data locally on your device to provide its functionality. This may include:

  • Your in-app preferences (such as dice colors and selected options)
  • Game state and roll history

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

3. Device Sensors

The App uses your device's motion sensors (accelerometer) to detect a shake gesture for rolling dice, and uses haptic feedback (vibration) for in-game effects. Sensor readings are processed in real time on your device and are not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

4. Permissions

The App requests only the minimum permissions needed for gameplay (for example, vibration). It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, files, or network beyond what the operating system requires.

5. Third-Party Services

Game Dice does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Game Dice is suitable for users of all ages. Because the App does not collect any personal information from anyone, it does not collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction).

7. Security

Because all data stays on your device, it is protected by the security mechanisms of your device and operating system. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
